Life Archive

Preserve your life milestones

Life Archive is a beautifully designed diary app that helps you preserve the meaningful moments that shape your life. From birth and childhood memories to education, travels, career milestones, weddings, parenthood, and even the bittersweet farewells—every chapter deserves to be remembered.

Record Every Life Event

Create entries for any significant moment—birth, school achievements, travel adventures, proposals, weddings, childbirth, career changes, family events, and more.

Never Forget a Date Again

Add specific dates to each event and keep your memories organized in a clear, chronological life timeline.

Add Details That Matter

Document locations, people involved, and personal reflections to bring each memory vividly to life.

Capture Photos & Videos

Attach images and videos to every entry and relive your most precious moments anytime.

Create Diaries for Loved Ones

Build a life archive for your child, baby, or even your beloved pet—so they can one day look back and cherish their early years.

Download & Save Memories

Export individual entries directly to your device—perfect for printing, scrapbooks, or sharing with loved ones.

Your Life, Beautifully Preserved

Life Archive transforms your memories into a visually rich, chronological journey. Whether you're documenting your own life or creating a keepsake for someone you love, this app ensures every story is remembered.

Start preserving your life’s milestones today—and keep every precious chapter forever.
